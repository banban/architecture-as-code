GDPR in Europe ()
Directive 95/46/EC The General Data Protection Regulation The GDPR 2016 contains 11 eleven chapters, concerning general provisions, principles, rights of the data subject, duties of data controllers or processors, transfers of personal data to third countries, supervisory authorities, cooperation among member states, remedies, liability or penalties for breach of rights, and miscellaneous final provisions.

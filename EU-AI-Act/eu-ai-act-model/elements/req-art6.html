Art. 06: Classification Rules ()
High-risk AI = (1) safety component or product under Annex I EU laws AND subject to third-party conformity assessment under those laws, OR (2) listed in Annex III use cases. Exception: AI performing narrow procedural task, improving prior human activity, detecting patterns without replacing human assessment, or preparatory tasks.

Art. 53: Obligations for All GPAI Providers ()
ALL GPAI model providers must: (1) Draw up and maintain technical documentation including training details and evaluation results. (2) Provide information/documentation to downstream providers to understand capabilities/limitations. (3) Establish copyright compliance policy (Copyright Directive 2019/790). (4) Publish summary of training content.

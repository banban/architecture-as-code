During a Strength, Weakness, Opportunities and Threat (SWOT) analysis it would become clear whether for example a culture is a threat, opportunity or both and needs to be balanced. Threat ID, description A.T.1 Human species are threatened by species of its own sort, due to conflict of interest and survival of the fittest; A.T.2 Human species are threatened by natural weather conditions, like heavy storms/hurricanes; floods; extreme temperatures; A.T.3 Human species are threatened by animals that are higher in the eco-system, or when human species become a threat to this animal and it will defend it self; A.T.4 Human species are threatened by environment conditions, like rocky or dessert areas, earthquakes, sea. Opportunity ID, description A.O.1 Human species will survive longer due to collaboration with each other to withstand threats; A.O.2 Human species will grow stronger due to collaboration for knowledge sharing between generations; A.O.3 Human species can improve quality of life when food and water supplies are preserved for quality and eatable, drinkable quality
